## Description
Magnet Station is a Safari extension that lets you send magnet links directly to your Synology NAS Download Station with a single click.

**Features:**
• One-click magnet link downloads - click any magnet link to instantly add it to Download Station
• View active downloads with real-time progress
• Connect via local IP or QuickConnect ID
• Copy magnet links from your download queue
• Quick access to your Synology web interface

**Easy Setup:**
1. Install the extension
2. Enter your Synology NAS address or QuickConnect ID
3. Log in with your credentials
4. Click any magnet link to start downloading

**Requirements:**
• Synology NAS with Download Station installed
• Safari on macOS or iOS

**Privacy:**
Your credentials are stored locally on your device and are only used to communicate with your own Synology NAS. No data is sent to third parties.
